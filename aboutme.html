Ala Vaikuntapuramlo

The movie Ala Vaikuntapuramlo is a 2020 Indian Telugu-language action drama film directed by Trivikram Srinivas, starring Allu Arjun and Pooja Hegde in the lead roles. It is one of the block buster movies in the year 2020. The main protagonist AlluArjun, delivers his best performance and also the background music and songs are exceptionally tremendous. I love this movie as it has wonderful story line, amazing screenplay and also its the best family entertainer.
